# Simplex Studio
[Home](index.md)

Simplex Studio is a boutique research and development entity focused on the intersection of **High-Performance Computing (HPC)** and **Scientific Computing**. 

## Our Mission
We believe that as AI-driven content generation evolves, the need for "Physically Grounded Truth" becomes paramount. We develop and accelerate solvers that provide a rigorous physical baseline for interactive environments, ensuring that digital worlds behave as users expect them to—without the need for thousands of manual parameters.
